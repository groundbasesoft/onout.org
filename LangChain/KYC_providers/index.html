Key Features to Look For in a KYC Provider

When evaluating KYC providers, key features to look for include:

  • Comprehensive identity verification - Support for identity documents, liveness detection, biometric matching, proof of address, etc.
  • AML and sanctions screening - Checking customers against watchlists and politically exposed persons (PEP) databases.
  • Automation - AI and machine learning to streamline workflows and improve match rates.
  • Customizable workflows - Flexible tools and integrations to build tailored identity verification journeys.
  • Compliance expertise - Ensuring solutions meet KYC, AML, GDPR and other regulatory requirements.
  • Global coverage - Ability to verify identities from over 200 countries and in various languages.
  • Secure data privacy - Encryption, access controls and compliance certifications to protect sensitive personal data.
